Vacation in Gytheio

Holidays in Gythio will become a trend this summer! And that's because firstly you go by vehicle and save the boat and secondly it has beautiful places to see.It has beautiful beaches with crystal clear waters but also many attractions! It is an ideal destination for families. Although Gythio offers a place to walk and eat all year round, the peak of its tourist season is the summer, so you can also visit the surrounding sandy beaches.

Gytheio Archaeological Places

In the gytheion you can see Marathonisi which is none other than the ancient Kranai where, according to mythology, Paris and Helen found refuge shortly before they left for Troy. You can still visit the theater that was built during the early Roman times, where performances are still held today.
